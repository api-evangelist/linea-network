Linea and Bermuda built a proof of concept for atomic delivery-versus-payment (DvP) settlement across chains while preserving privacy and trustlessness, combining Linea's Lineth zkEVM stack with Bermuda's privacy layer. Both transaction legs complete together or not at all, eliminating principal risk between counterparties while keeping transactions private yet cryptographically verifiable. The framework targets tokenized securities, tokenized deposits, and regulated stablecoin-like bank money, and extends to payment-versus-payment for FX and other institutional settlement workflows.
